FAQ

Got questions?

We've tried to cover the most common ones. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to Olivia or Caroline.

Is attendance mandatory?
This offsite is an important part of how we build a strong team and culture at Encube. It's key for alignment, learning and collaboration across the company. While we understand that exceptional circumstances can arise, we expect everyone to join.
Want to come earlier or stay longer?
That's totally fine, extra nights are on you. Hotel is covered from Sunday night (April 19) to checkout on Friday (April 24). Interested in extending your stay? We'll send out a form where you can share your input, and we'll reserve the room for you.
When do I need to arrive? And when can I book my return flight?
Arrival: Sunday, April 19, we'll start first thing Monday morning.

Departure: Friday, April 24, in the afternoon. The offsite ends around lunchtime.
How and when should I book my travel?
You'll book your own flights. Just make sure to save the receipts (with VAT included). And be reasonable with costs when booking.
What will the agenda look like (work vs. social time)?
We know you're curious! 👀 We're working on this and will let you know as soon as possible.
Who should I contact if I have questions?
Olivia or Caroline 💪🏼
How do expenses and reimbursements work?
Swedish employees: Submit flight expenses with valid receipts (including VAT where applicable). Approved expenses will be reimbursed mid-month / in connection with the regular payroll run.

Everyone else: Add the expenses to your monthly invoice and we pay the expenses as part of your invoice payment. We still need a scan of the receipt and it needs to be a VAT receipt.
